{\bf Theorem: 1.1-1}
\vskip 1mm
\hrule

\vskip 6pt
For each event $A$,

$$P(A)=1-P(A')$$

\vskip 10pt
{\bf Proof}

\vskip 6pt
We have

$$S=A\cup A'\quad\hbox{and}\quad A\cap A'=\emptyset$$

Thus, from properties (b) and (c) of {\bf probability}, it follows

$$1=P(A)+P(A')$$

Hence

$$P(A)=1-P(A')$$

\vfill\eject
