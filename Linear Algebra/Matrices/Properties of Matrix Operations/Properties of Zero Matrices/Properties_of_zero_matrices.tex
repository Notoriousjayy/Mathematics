{\bf Properties of Zero Matrices}
\vskip 1mm
\hrule

\vskip 6pt

If $A$ is an $m\times n$ matric and $c$ is a scalar, then the properties below are true.

\vskip 10pt
1. $A+0_{mn}=A$

\vskip 6pt
2. $A+(-A)=0_{mn}$

\vskip 6pt
3. If $cA=0_{mn}$, then $c=0$ or $A=0_{mn}$

\vfill\eject
