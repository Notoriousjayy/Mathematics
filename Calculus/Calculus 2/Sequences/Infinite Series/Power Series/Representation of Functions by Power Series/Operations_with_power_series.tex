{\bf Operations with Power Series}
\vskip 6pt
Let f(x)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^n \quad\hbox{and}\; g(x)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty b_nx^n$
$$1. \;\;\; f(kx)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nk^nx^n$$
$$2. \;\;\; f(x^N)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nx^{nN}$$
$$3. \;\;\; f(x)\pm g(x)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty (a_n \pm b_n)x^n$$
